Rwanda's Economy Projected to Grow by 7.1% in 2025

Finance Minister Yusuf Murangwa announced that Rwanda's economy is expected to grow at a rate of 7.1% in 2025, down from 8.9% in 2024. The government has proposed a draft budget of 7.03 trillion Rwandan francs ($5.01 billion) for the 2025/26 fiscal year, focusing on agriculture, tourism, and manufacturing sectors.
