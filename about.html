About Us

AmbiVision Rwanda Holdings is a private investment group established in 2021. We identify and grow high-potential ventures that offer unique solutions in critical sectors.

We are driven by innovation, strategy, and measurable impact. Through our subsidiaries—spanning technology, agriculture, aviation, and green industries—we invest in businesses that shape Africa’s future.

AmbiVision is fully owned by AmbiVision Capital Inc., registered in Delaware, USA. With local insight and global support, we turn bold ideas into powerful ventures.
